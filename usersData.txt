Profession:
    HAIR STYLIST
			A Hair Stylist is a professional who specializes in creating and designing hairstyles, haircuts, and hairdos to accentuate a client's individuality.
    MAKEUP ARTIST
			A Makeup Artist enhances facial aesthetics through makeup, creating custom looks for clients.
    ESTHETICIAN
			An esthetician is a skincare expert who performs treatments to enhance and maintain skin health and beauty.
    NAIL TECHNICIAN
			A Nail Technician is a professional who beautifies and maintains the health of clients' nails through manicures, pedicures, and other nail treatments.
    EYELESH SPECIALIST
			An eyelash specialist, also known as a lash artist, enhances natural lashes through techniques like eyelash extensions, lifts, and tints.
    MASSAGE THERAPIST
			A massage therapist provides relaxation or treats body pain through physical touch, manipulating muscles. They customize massages based on client needs.
    TATTOO ARTIST
			Tattoo artists use needles and inks to create permanent tattoos on clients’ bodies, adhering to health regulations.
